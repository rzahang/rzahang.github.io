About me

i am a professional web designer

Drawing from my thorough understanding of user-centric design, I have completed or assisted with completing several projects as a User Experience and User Interface Designer. My duties included researching technology and interaction design trends and collaborating with experience architects to develop solid user experience planning and troubleshooting design problems.

I love fashion design and enjoy spending time sewing and making my own clothes.

I spend enormous amount of time drawing, taking photos, painting, and exploring places. Other times, I'm working on becoming a better design influencer.

I'm interested in designing meaningful experiences that shape how we interact with technology and moving businesses forward with a user-experience-centric approach.
